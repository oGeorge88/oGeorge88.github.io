Understanding VAT Calculation

When purchasing accessories, it's important to understand the cost breakdown. We use a Value Added Tax (VAT) system to ensure transparency in pricing.

Gross Price: This is the total price you pay for an accessory, including VAT.

VAT: The tax amount calculated based on a percentage of the net price. Our standard VAT rate is 7%.

Net Price: The price of the accessory before VAT is added. You can calculate it using the formula:

Net Price = Gross Price / (1 + VAT Rate)

For example, if an accessory costs 100 BAHT (Gross Price) and the VAT rate is 7%, the Net Price and VAT amount are calculated as follows:

VAT Amount = Gross Price / (100 + VAT Rate) * VAT Rate

Net Price = Gross Price - VAT Amount
